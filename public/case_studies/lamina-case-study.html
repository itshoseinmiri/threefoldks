01 · Problem before Lamina
“

Fabrication models live in formats the web can't open — every review needed a licensed CAD seat, and printability was judged by eye.

  • ×Reviewers couldn't open IFC/STEP without desktop software.
  • ×Wall thickness, overhangs & Z-limit checked manually.
  • ×No shared place to comment, version or compare iterations.
  • ×Slicing meant exporting to yet another tool.
Late failures
caught after slicing
02 · Solution one browser tab
“

The review used to happen over email and screenshots. Now everyone opens the same live model, and the printability verdict is right there before slicing.

Ingest & view

Any of 6 formats → streaming glTF, 11 render modes, live dimensions.

Analyze & fabricate

31-check evaluation, AI segmentation, slicing & remeshing — built in.
